# Product Support-Path Matrix: Round 08

Status: Approved by four leads
Date: 2026-06-11

| Support Lane | User Question | Next Action | Trust / Safety Wording | Commitment Risk |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| NFC tag or pet profile | A scan did not open the expected pet profile, or the finder page/contact state is unclear. | `Shop or replace a tag` -> `/store`; include tag ID and owner contact state in support email. | Emphasizes tag ID and owner-shared contact details. | Does not promise support can override owner privacy or lost-mode settings. |
| Store or order help | A user has a Smart Tag, checkout, or order-status question. | `View store` -> `/store`; include order email, product name, and checkout/success screen. | Keeps commerce help grounded in existing store/order context. | Does not change checkout, order, Stripe, or support backend behavior. |
| Account and community | A user needs help with login, posting, chat, or moderation. | `Email support` -> `mailto:hello@pawpal.be`. | Requests account email and affected screen only. | Does not introduce a ticketing SLA, moderation promise, or account workflow change. |
| Safety or urgent pet issue | A pet safety situation may be urgent. | `Open Globe` -> `/globe` after local services first. | Explicitly says local services first, then PawPal owner/community context. | Avoids implying PawPal is an emergency-response service. |

## Product Decision

Round 08 improves Help route clarity and support routing without adding new backend support behavior, new policy/legal/support promises, new product journeys, or commerce/account/map behavior changes.
